  • Child & Adult Protective Services

    Emergency: To report child abuse or neglect, call (419) 563-1570 (24/7). After hours, call 911. For adult abuse (age 60+), call 567-393-4729.

    Child Protective Services

    • Phone: (419) 563-1570 (24/7)
    • Fax: 419-562-0050
    • Office: 224 Norton Way, Bucyrus, OH 44820
    • Hours: Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM–4:00 PM

    Mission: To improve the safety, permanency, and well-being of children and families involved in the Child Welfare system through collaboration with families and their community.

    How to Report: Call Crawford County Child Protective Services at (419) 563-1570 and state you wish to make a child abuse and neglect report. Reports can be made 24/7.

    Confidentiality: The law protects the confidentiality of a reporter. A social worker cannot reveal, confirm or deny who a reporter may be. There are no consequences for reports made in good faith, even if unfounded.

    Adult Protective Services

    Adult Protective Services staff investigate reports of abuse, neglect (including self-neglect) or financial exploitation of adults age 60 and over who are unable to protect themselves due to a serious physical or mental limitation.

    • Phone: 567-393-4729
    • Partnership: Works with The Ohio District 5 Area Agency on Aging

    Types of Elder Abuse:

    • Neglect: Caretakers failing to provide food, shelter, healthcare, or protection
    • Self-Neglect: Elderly individuals unable or unwilling to provide necessary self-care
    • Exploitation: Unauthorized use or theft of an elder's funds or property
    • Emotional Abuse: Verbal threats, intimidation, name-calling, harassment and isolation
    • Physical Abuse: Injury inflicted through hitting, kicking, or weaponry
    • Sexual Abuse: Nonconsensual or forced sexual contact

    Foster Care & Adoption

    Help change a child's life…become a foster and/or adoptive parent

    Fostering a child and/or providing a forever home at a time they need it most can have a lifelong impact. There are several children in the custody of Crawford County Child Protective Services that need the love and care that you can provide.

    • Contact: Beth Sergent at 567-393-4733
    • Requirements: Single or married, homeowners or renters, age 21+. Must be licensed with ongoing training and criminal check required.
    • Resources: fosterandadopt.jfs.ohio.gov

    Kinship Care

    Support and resources available for relatives caring for children. Contact (419) 562-0015 for more information.

    Safe Haven for Newborns

    Birth parents may leave an infant (up to 30 days old) with a medical worker at a hospital, emergency medical worker, or law enforcement officer without prosecution if the infant has not been abused.
