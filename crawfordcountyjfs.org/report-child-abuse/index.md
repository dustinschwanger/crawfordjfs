# Report Child Abuse – Crawford County JFS

## Overview

Crawford County Child Protective Services accepts abuse and neglect reports 24/7/365.

## Primary Contact

**Phone:** 419-563-1570
**Office Hours:** Monday through Friday 7:30 am to 4:00 pm

## Information to Provide When Reporting

When filing a report, individuals should supply:
- Child's name and address
- Child's age
- Parent/guardian name and address
- Suspected abuser's name
- Reason for suspicion
- Any additional relevant details

## Key Protections

Reports remain confidential. Your identity cannot be released to anyone without your permission, except under direct order from the court.

Ohio law shields good-faith reporters from civil or criminal liability. Anonymous reports are accepted.

## Mandated Reporters

Per Ohio Revised Code 2151-421, mandated reporters (educators, healthcare professionals, social workers, childcare providers, law enforcement) must report suspected abuse or neglect of children under 18 or physically impaired youth under 21.
