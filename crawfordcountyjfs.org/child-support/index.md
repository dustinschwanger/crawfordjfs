# Child Support – Crawford County JFS

## Key Service Areas
- Paternity establishment
- Child/Medical support order establishment
- Order enforcement
- Review and adjustment of orders
- Payment processing
- Employment assistance

## Services Overview

Paternity must be established prior to establishing a child / medical support order. Applicants can access relevant forms online.

The agency enforces orders through:
- Income withholding
- License suspension
- Asset seizure
- Tax refund interception
- Other legal mechanisms

## Payment Options

- Direct bank transfers
- Credit/debit cards via Ohio CSPC (1-888-965-2676)
- Mail payments
- In-person payments at the agency office
