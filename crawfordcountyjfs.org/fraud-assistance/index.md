# Crawford County Job and Family Services - Fraud Assistance

## Overview

Crawford County Job and Family Services takes allegations of public assistance fraud seriously.

## What to Report

The organization encourages community members to report suspected fraud involving:
- Medicaid
- Food Assistance
- Ohio Works First (cash assistance and workforce development programs)

## How to Report

The agency provides a link to make fraud reports through the state system.
