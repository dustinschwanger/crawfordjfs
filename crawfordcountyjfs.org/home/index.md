## Main Services Offered

The organization provides several key service areas:

- **OhioMeansJobs:** Supporting community success through work and training with assistance in jobs, training, and job preparation
- **Child/Adult Protective Services:** Including child abuse reporting
- **Foster Care & Adoption Services**
- **Public Assistance:** Online applications available at benefits.ohio.gov
- **Child Support Enforcement**
