## Services Listed

The page identifies four protective service areas:

1. **Adult Protective Services** – For reporting elder abuse, neglect, or exploitation
2. **Kinship** – Support for relatives caring for children
3. **Ongoing Case Services**
4. **Reporting Child Abuse or Neglect** – 24/7 reporting hotline available
