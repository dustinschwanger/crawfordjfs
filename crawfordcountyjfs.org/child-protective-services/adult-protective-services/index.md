## Overview

Crawford County Job and Family Services partners with The Ohio District 5 Area Agency on Aging (AAA) to administer Adult Protective Services. This program began in October 2018.

## Services

Highly trained and specialized AAA Adult Protective Services Case Workers handle reports made to the Crawford County Job and Family Services for alleged elder abuse, neglect or exploitation.

The program takes a proactive approach to maintain safety and independence as people age. AAA staff connect individuals with community resources and programs tailored to their specific needs, working collaboratively with local partners to address complex situations.
