# Public Assistance – Crawford County JFS

## Overview

Crawford County's Public Assistance services provide support to residents in need.

## Service Categories
- Food Assistance (SNAP)
- Medical Assistance
- Cash Assistance
- Fraud Assistance
- PRC & NET Transportation
- OhioMeansJobs
- State Hearings
