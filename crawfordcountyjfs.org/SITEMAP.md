## Key Services Offered

1. **Employment Services** - Job search, training, business services
2. **Child Protective Services** - 24/7 abuse reporting and investigation
3. **Adult Protective Services** - Elder abuse reporting and support
4. **Foster Care & Adoption** - Foster parent recruitment and adoption services
5. **Kinship Care** - Support for relatives caring for children
6. **Public Assistance** - SNAP, Medicaid, cash assistance
7. **Child Support** - Paternity establishment, order enforcement, payment processing
8. **Community Resources** - Connections to local services and organizations
